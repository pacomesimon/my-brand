About

Hi! I am Pacome Simon Mbonimpa, a web design trainee. My name, Pacome is a boy's name of French origin meaning "of strong nature, or, pacifier". Pacome is a French saint's name rarely heard in this country, but currently being revived in its native France. Some variants are Pacomi, Pacomio and Pacomius.

Regarding my education background, I completed my undergraduate studies in faculty of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, at the University of Rwanda. As part of academic progression, I did an internship as an IT and electronics fellow in Enviroserve Rwanda Green Park Ltd., an electronic hardware recycling company.

As it can be seen on my Linkedin profile, I completed projects like “Implementation of a Sentiment Analyzer using IBM’s Watson API” and “Machine Learning predictions of Taxi Pickups using Matlab”. I participated in IBM’s 2019 hackathon called “Master the Mainframe”, and achieved a regional winner award.

Having experienced the importance of programming in today’s world, I am determined to train and shape myself into a good cloud developer, starting from basic web development up to current/trending/emerging cloud technologies.
